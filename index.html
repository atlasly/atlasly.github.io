What we do

We provide geospatial and temporal data visualization services using the latest web technologies. We can make sense of a variety of data sets, even large ones. We handle both real-time data as well as historical data. Then we craft them into beautiful and intuitive interfaces with our UX expertise.

Hire us for your geospatial project today!

Our Services

  • 2D/3D GIS solutions

    We provide visualization solutions using the latest web standards and technologies such as CesiumJS, ThreeJS, MapBox, Leaflet, and Google Maps to fit the customers exact needs.

  • Geospatial data visualization

    Using a variety of technologies we are able to accomplish visualizing geospatial data points on a 3d globe or 2d map, whether it be a few data points or 100,000+.

  • Temporal data visualization

    Experience time travel as we are experts in visualizing changes in data over time as well as providing rich interfaces to give the user the control of it all.

  • Graph/Charts

    We also provide visualizations of data in graphs and charts if it doesn't make sense to be on a GIS platform. We typically do this side by side with a geospatial visualization. We use tools such as D3 and Vis.js to accomplish the desired affect.

  • 3D model visualization

    We can bring in 3D models into our geospatial visualizations. 3D buildings is our area of expertise.

  • UX design expertise

    We have years of experience in UX/UI design and the design process. We leverage that experience to provide our clients with solutions that not only have rich intuitive interfaces but also perfectly fits their needs.

  • AWS/Google Cloud Setup

    We can setup your web application and web services on AWS or Google Cloud.

  • Drone Imagery

    We are license drone pilots. We can take drone imagery to create 3D models of surroundings as well as 2d imagery for maps.
